ABOUT US

Empower EIT presents differentiated service opportunities in next-generation
lithium production and advanced energy technologies.

Through proprietary Direct Lithium Extraction (DLE) and conversion technologies, combined with large-scale lithium resources, Empower is building a fully vertically integrated platform spanning technology, development, and critical mineral assets.

Empower is among the first companies to offer a true end-to-end lithium solution, integrating both concentration and refining processes within a single system. With more than 12 years of dedicated research and development, Empowers proprietary technology enables efficient, scalable, and environmentally responsible lithium production.

Positioned at the forefront of the global energy transition, Empower offers investors compelling risk-adjusted return potential while supporting evolving domestic and international sustainability priorities. Through innovation, operational expertise, and strategic resource development, Empower is helping secure the future supply of critical materials essential for electrification and clean energy.

Mission: Empower EIT’s mission is to advance the global energy transition by building a fully vertically integrated lithium platform. Through the development and deployment of proprietary Direct Lithium Extraction (DLE) and conversion technologies, combined with integrated project development and processing, Empower EIT is creating a secure, efficient, and environmentally responsible supply of lithium for the electrification economy.

Select projects

SALTON SEA / HELLS KITCHEN

Provided R&D support for multiple projects in the Salton Sea geothermal area with successful bench scale lithium recovery using Empowers
DLE through to a pre-commercial pilot implementation in a 400 tpa plant. Participated
or supported all aspects of engineering and development with the provision oif over 40 tons of our DLE media.

ALBERTA, CanADA

Joint venture resource project advancing Direct Lithium Extraction testing toward pilot-scale deployment. Empower is supporting pilot design and validation, with ownership milestones tied to performance and commercialization targets. DLE through to a pre-commercial pilot implementation in a 400 tpa plant. Participated or supported all aspects of engineering and development with the provision oif over 40 tons of our DLE media.

SMACKOVER

Joint venture resource project advancing Direct Multiple bench scale studies to evaluate feasibility. Empower’s DLE excels in high H2S and barium containing brines with industry leading selectivity for lithium. Third-party validation of Empowers DLE process. Project development pending client funding of asset development.

BARNETT

Multiple bench trials led to 4-month on-site pilot on a poor-quality brine. Successfully out-competed other trialed technologies with minimal pre-treatment requirements. Projects pending improved lithium pricing.

East Texas

Multiple successful bench trials validating Empower’s DLE technology. Finalist in a competition spearheaded by a Texas oil supermajor assessing the best global technologies. Like the Barnett, these brines are poor quality and not economic until lithium pricing recovers.

Nevada

Successful bench scale validation of technology for greenfield brine resources proximate to well-known existing resources. Asset development pending.

Utah

Successful bench scale validation of Empowers DLE technology using from a mineral mine evaporation pond. Submission of a project development design and proposal, however, current seasonal operation hinders feasibility.

Marcellus

Processing frac-flowback concentrates from a water treatment plant. Multiple bench trials led to on-site pilot. One of the lowest quality waters trialed with respect to lithium to divalent ratios and our DLE performed.

Alberta, CA

Bench scale validation on evaporator concentrates from steam assisted oil recovery, multiple brown and greenfield projects on low lithium brines with exceptional performance. Extensive laboratory and technical support for project development that
is on-going.

Saskatchewan, CA

Two greenfield projects with extensive bench validation of the Empower DLE technology. One JV in place for 30% ownership as milestones are
achieved. Pilot system build and deployment pending project funding.
